*Time moves slow when you are standing still and moves normally when you move.
*Enemies will be shooting at the player, but the player won’t be able to shoot backunless 
they collect ammo
*By positioning yourself at a particular position, you can make enemies kill each other.

Hot To Play:
*Press the left arrow to rotate left and the right arrow to rotate right.
*Move out of the way by either pressing up arrow to move forward or down 
arrow to move backwards.
*When you have collected blocks dropped by enemies when the die, your health replenishes.

Enjoy!
